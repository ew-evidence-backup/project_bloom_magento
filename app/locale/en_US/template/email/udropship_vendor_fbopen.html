Here are some tips on how to maximize the value of your store:
Make your Facebook Store Visible

If you already have several apps on your business Facebook page, the Facebook store may be hidden. Click the down arrow directly on the right of your photos and other Facebook apps and hover your mouse over your flower shop's Facebook store. Click the symbol in the top right corner and switch your Facebook store with one of your other apps so that customers can start shopping.
Let your Fans Know

Post on your wall that customers can now shop directly with you on your Facebook page.  In your post, copy and paste your Facebook Store URL.  After you post it, you can delete the link and put in your own marketing material.
Offer Promotions

Offer discounts on their next order or free delivery for anyone that likes or shares your Facebook store posts. When your fans share your Facebook store it is like having them sharing your entire catalog to all their friends and family with the click of a button.
Update Your Catalogue

When you add new bouquets to your BloomNation profile, they also sync with your Facebook store.  Post your new designs on your wall and keep your customers up to date with the latest floral trends.
